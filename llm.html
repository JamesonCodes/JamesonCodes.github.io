What this site is

This is the portfolio and writing site of Jameson Campbell, a Sales Operations Manager and Internal Tools Builder. The site focuses on building AI-powered tools that multiply team productivity, rapid prototyping, and systems integration.

Content covers internal tools development, AI pair programming, automation workflows, customer experience systems, and practical frameworks for scaling operations. The site is designed for teams and individuals looking to build leverage through automation and AI.
