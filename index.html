About Me

I bridge the gap between operations and engineering.

I'm most energized when I can talk to a teammate, spot a friction point, and ship something that fixes it.

I started in Sales Operations but quickly found myself building apps, bots, and data pipelines to automate repetitive work. Today, I'm focused on building tools that multiply people — internal search systems, triage assistants, and micro-apps that remove bottlenecks.

My approach:

  • → Start small, test with real users, iterate fast.
  • → Measure what matters (adoption, time saved).
  • → Kill what doesn't work. Scale what does.

I'm currently exploring the intersection of internal tooling and AI augmentation, and building public proof-of-concepts to sharpen my craft.
