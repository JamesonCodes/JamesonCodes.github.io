About Me

I bridge the gap between operations and engineering.

Every company has craft. AI scales it. The future belongs to organizations where employees capture their expertise and distribute it across the company. Teams that marry craft and code will define the next frontier.

I'm most energized when I can shadow a teammate in their natural workflow, spot the friction they've learned to ignore, and ship a tool that removes it.

By title, I'm a Sales Operations Manager, but I've been building tech for over a decade. I graduated from the University of Texas at Austin with a degree in Economics & Business, completed UT's full-stack web development bootcamp, and spent 13 years in startups across multiple industries, shipping products like a top-grossing #13 U.S. Health & Fitness app.

Today, I'm focused on building tools that multiply people: internal search systems, triage assistants, and micro-apps that remove bottlenecks.

I use AI pair-programming, rapid prototyping, and modern cloud tooling to turn ideas into working systems fast. Whether I'm using Python or TypeScript, building in AWS or GCP, I rely on agents, observability, and iteration over perfection.

By prioritizing revealed behavior over stated needs, I ensure I'm building for the 20% of signals that drive 80% of the results. This prevents "feature bloat" and ensures the tools I build actually stick.

My goal: build leverage. Tools that make 10 people 10× faster.
